Peanut Butter Banana Ice Cream

Description:

Delicious and thick banana-based ice cream with peanut butter.

Ingredients:

  • 3 bananas
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp vanilla

Directions:

  1. Slice bananas into 1" slices.
  2. Place on parchment paper on a baking sheet and freeze for 2h.
  3. Blend frozen slices until smooth.
  4. Combine with remaining ingredients.
  5. Transfer to a container and freeze for 1h.
  6. Enjoy!
