Chicken Wings

Description:

Sweet, sticky, simple chicken wings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mustard
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 5lbs chicken wings

Instructions:

  1. Mix sauces and brown sugar. Move 1/4 cup to a bowl for basting; cover and refrigerate.
  2. Pour remaining sauce into a ziploc bag. Add wings, mix, and squeeze out air. Marinate for a minimum of 2 hours.
  3. When ready to cook, preheat oven to 375F. Line a baking tray with foil.
  4. Transfer wings to the baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 30m, baste with reserved marinade every 10m. Cook until chicken is white and juices are clear, aim for 165F.
